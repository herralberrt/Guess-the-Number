Guess the Number - Game Instructions:

1. Run the game using: python3 "Guess the Number.py"
2. Guess a number between 1 and 100.
3. If your guess is too high or too low, you'll get hints.
4. Your score decreases with each incorrect guess.
5. Try to guess the number in 5 attempts or less to get a bonus!

Good luck and have fun!
